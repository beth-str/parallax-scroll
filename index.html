Every Eye Will See Him

Look, he is coming with the clouds,
and every eye will see him…

Revelation 1:7

The sun turned black…
the whole moon turned blood red,
and the stars in the sky fell to earth…

Revelation 6:12-13

Then will appear the sign
of the Son of Man in heaven.

Matthew 24:30

At that time people will see
the Son of Man coming in clouds
with great power and glory.

Mark 13:26

 

They will wage war against the Lamb,
but the Lamb will triumph over them
because he is Lord of lords and King of kings
and with him will be his called,
chosen and faithful followers.

Revelation 17:14

He who testifies to these things says,
“Yes, I am coming soon.”
Amen. Come, Lord Jesus.
